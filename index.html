🧳 Travel
Gulliver Travels
Your personal travel command center

A trip organizer built to replace TripIt — forward your confirmation emails and they automatically become a clean, day-by-day itinerary. Great for solo travel or planning trips with others.

Open app ↗
What it does
  • Forward booking emails or even photos of a reservation and Gulliver Travels automatically places them in your itinerary — flights, hotels, restaurants, and more
  • Day-by-day view with weather, TSA wait times, and airport info
  • Share and sync trips with travel companions
  • Easily create and share a curated list of your favorite restaurants for any destination
  • Packing helper — get a smart packing list tailored to your trip and destinations
  • Subscribe to a calendar feed so your trips show up in Apple Calendar or Google Calendar automatically
How to get started
  1. 1Go to gullivertravels.app and sign up with your Google account or email address.
  2. 2Once you're in, add your first trip — give it a name and travel dates.
  3. 3Forward any booking confirmation email to GulliverTravelsPlans@gmail.com and watch it appear in your itinerary automatically.
  4. 4Explore the trip view — check weather, nearby places, and share your itinerary with anyone traveling with you.
  5. 5Add Gulliver Travels to your phone's home screen — tap Share then "Add to Home Screen" on iPhone, or the menu on Android. It'll open full-screen just like a native app.
🍽️ Food
Nightly Bites
A meal journal for people who love food

Track the meals you've made, save recipes you love, and get dinner ideas when you're drawing a blank. Built for a small group — everyone's entries are shared, so it becomes a living record of meals enjoyed together.

Open app ↗
What it does
  • Log meals with photos, ratings, and notes
  • Community Cookbook — recipes anyone in the group has saved, all in one place
  • Paste a recipe URL and a clean, formatted version is saved instantly
  • Dinner suggestion feature — tell it what's in your fridge, get ideas back
  • Your personal recipe box for anything you want to keep handy
How to get started
  1. 1Go to nightly-bites-web.vercel.app and sign up with your Google account or email address.
  2. 2Log your first meal — add a photo, a rating, and a few notes about what you made or ate.
  3. 3Browse the Community Cookbook to see what others in the group have been cooking and saving.
  4. 4Try the recipe saver — paste any recipe URL and it'll be cleaned up and added to your recipe box instantly.
  5. 5Add Nightly Bites to your home screen — tap Share then "Add to Home Screen" on iPhone, or the menu on Android. It opens full-screen with no browser in the way.
💳 Finance
Point-Taken
Know exactly where your money goes

A personal finance tracker that connects securely to your bank accounts and credit cards, automatically syncing transactions so you always have a clear picture of your spending — without the complexity of apps like Mint.

Open app ↗
What it does
  • All credit card and bank transactions merged into a single unified view — one place to search and find any charge
  • Clean spending breakdown by category, merchant, and card
  • Tracks your credit card benefits — which perks are available and which you've already used
  • Track recurring expenses to monitor subscriptions
  • "Which card should I use?" — recommendations based on your actual cards and rewards
How to get started
  1. 1Go to point-taken-tan.vercel.app and sign up with your Google account or email address.
  2. 2Create a SimpleFIN account ($1.50/month or $15/year) — this is the secure bridge that connects your banks without sharing your passwords.
  3. 3In SimpleFIN, connect your banks and credit cards, then generate a setup token and paste it into Point-Taken. One-time setup.
  4. 4Your transactions will start syncing automatically. Explore your spending breakdown, set up your cards, and check which benefits you still have available.
  5. 5Add Point-Taken to your home screen — tap Share then "Add to Home Screen" on iPhone, or the menu on Android — for quick access anytime you want to check your spending.
